Professional Background

After years of connecting people to solutions, I’m excited to create solutions myself. I have a background in sales and management, where I honed my problem-solving skills and adaptability. Transitioning into backend development has been an exciting journey!

I recently served as a Data Annotation Specialist at Tesla, recognized as "Data Labeler of the Week" for my commitment to quality and efficiency. Before that, I spent a decade with Vector Marketing, where I earned the "Annual Incentive Trip" three times and ranked as a top 10 branch manager. I had the privilege of helping run the #1 new business office in the nation in the fall of 2017.

In addition to my sales experience, I've developed a range of technical skills. I'm most confident in Python and Git, and I've worked on projects including a Pokédex game using the PokeAPI and an Asteroids game with Python and Pygame. I also built a system for analyzing large datasets during my time earning a Python Fundamentals certificate from Cornell, and I created a Spell Scraper using JavaScript to parse HTML into usable JSON.

With 13 certificates from Boot.dev and a Python certificate from Cornell, I'm eager to explore data management, AI, and machine learning. My goal is to become a Senior Developer, mentoring new developers along the way. I pride myself on clear communication, hard work, and problem-solving abilities, always striving for improvement!
